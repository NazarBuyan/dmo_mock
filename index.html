Top Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about visiting Dubai

The best time to visit Dubai is from November to March when the weather is pleasant with temperatures ranging from 24°C to 35°C. Summer months (June to September) can be extremely hot with temperatures exceeding 40°C.
Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Citizens of many countries including the US, UK, EU, Australia, and others can get a visa on arrival. It's best to check the official UAE government website for the most current information.
Dubai is known for its luxury accommodations. Top hotels include Burj Al Arab, Atlantis The Palm, One&Only Royal Mirage, Armani Hotel Dubai, and Four Seasons Resort Dubai at Jumeirah Beach.
Desert safaris are a popular way to experience Dubai's desert. These typically include dune bashing, camel riding, sandboarding, and a BBQ dinner with entertainment. Many tour operators offer half-day or full-day desert experiences.
